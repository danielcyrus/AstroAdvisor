Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Astrology Advisor application ("App"). Astrology Advisor ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our App. This Privacy Policy explains our information practices and the choices available to you regarding the use of your information.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the Astrology Advisor App, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, birth date, birth time, birth location
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, time spent on features, interactions with the App
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Astrological Preferences: Zodiac sign interests, reading preferences, favorite features
  • AI Training Data: Your interactions with AI features to improve personalization and accuracy
⚠️ Important Note: We do NOT collect sensitive personal information such as health records, financial information, or real-time location data unless explicitly required for core functionality of the astrological services.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively within our App for the following purposes:

  • Providing personalized astrological readings and horoscopes
  • Creating and analyzing your birth chart
  • Calculating compatibility readings and transit predictions
  • Improving our AI algorithms to deliver more accurate and personalized insights
  • Enhancing App functionality and user experience
  • Maintaining account security and preventing fraud
  • Responding to customer support inquiries
✓ Your Data Privacy Guarantee:

We DO NOT share, sell, rent, or transfer your personal information to any third parties, including advertisers, data brokers, business partners, or other external organizations. Your data remains confidential and is used exclusively within our App to provide you with better astrological guidance and AI-powered insights.

4. AI-Powered Services and Machine Learning

Our App uses artificial intelligence to provide personalized astrological insights. Your interaction data is used to:

  • Train and improve our AI models for more accurate readings
  • Personalize recommendations based on your preferences
  • Enhance the accuracy of birth chart and transit predictions
  • Refine compatibility analysis algorithms

This machine learning training occurs entirely within our secure infrastructure. Your data is never shared with external AI providers or third-party machine learning platforms.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Encryption of data at rest
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Restricted access to personal information (need-to-know basis)

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@astroadvisor.app. Upon deletion, we will remove your data from our active systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Data Portability: Request to download your data in a standard format
  • Opt-Out: Withdraw consent for certain uses of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@astroadvisor.app with your request.

8. Third-Party Services

Our App may contain links to third-party websites and services that are not operated by us. This Privacy Policy applies only to information we collect through our App. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

Important: We do not share your information with third-party services for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose without your explicit consent.

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. Your continued use of the App following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.
